PRODUCTS

Each of our thoughtfully-designed and intelligently-engineered products is available one a stand-alone basis or as part of Indira’s integrated personalized retailing platform

IndiraFIT

70% of ecommerce clothing returns are due to problems with fit and sizing. End those problems with IndiraFit.

IndiraFit uses sophisticated computer vision technology to capture precise measurements of users right from their webcam or mobile device.

Direct data outputs are ideal for custom clothing makers, subscription companies, online brands & fitness applications. Combined with our unparalleled ability to generate accurate product specs, IndiraFit is the ideal way for any e-retailer to recommend the perfect size or best-fitting brand to each individual customer.

IndiraCOLOR

As a retailer, you know the profound impact color has on sales. Stocking the colors your customers want and displaying the colors that will grab their eye is key, and now you can do that perfectly for every one of your customers.

IndiraColor is a visual search technology that understands the real-time color preferences of each individual customer by mathematically analyzing their visual interactions online.

We learn exactly which colors a customer is most likely to respond to, and help you put products in the right color in front of each customer in email campaigns, on your storefront, and in social media campaigns. You can even use IndiraColor to help allocate your physical inventory to your retail stores.

IndiraSTYLE

Knowing each customer’s style is how the very best personal shoppers outperform sales goals year after year. Bring the power of a million personal shoppers to your ecommerce site with IndiraStyle.

IndiraStyle uses celebrity photos in a fun, engaging game backed up by adaptive learning algorithms to determine each customer’s personal style preferences. Customers have fun, and you gain the ability to understand their style and blow them away with personalized product suggestions.
